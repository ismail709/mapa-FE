MAPA examination center

“MAPA, a Part 147 approved centre, organizes 12 Part 66 exam sessions per year for all the licenses covered by its approval.


These exam sessions are open to anyone wishing to obtain the theoretical modules of the Part 66 B1.1 or B2 licenses as an independent candidate.


The exams take place on CBT (computer based training) or in writing and the candidates have the possibility of taking exams according to their aeronautical titles or diplomas recognized by Civil Aviation.


The examination sessions comply with the requirements of the European Commission regulations implementing the licensing of maintenance personnel.

Preparation for the exam

Revisions are provided to candidates within MAPA in order to best prepare for the exams.

Examination process

On the day of the exam, you must bring an identity document and the signed exam application form. During the event, only the documents and materials that will be given to you may be used. No other documents or materials will be allowed. The results will be sent to candidates no later than 10 days after the examination. No results will be communicated the same day.

Notice

The exam may be canceled if registrations prove ineffective. Any registration request will only be validated upon receipt of the financial payment.
